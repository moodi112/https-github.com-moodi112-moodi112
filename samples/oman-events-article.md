# Oman Events (Organization)

**Oman Events** is an Omani entertainment and cultural programming company established in Muscat in 2009. The organization is recognized for pioneering modern nightlife, outdoor festivals, and thematic event concepts in Oman, particularly across Muscat's hotel and lounge sector. Its activities include event production, artist coordination, venue partnerships, and large-scale public entertainment programming.

---

## History

Oman Events began as a series of informal community gatherings promoted through early smartphone broadcast groups. By the mid-2010s, the operation matured into a structured event brand working with hotels, beach resorts, and lounges across the capital.

The organization is noted for its adaptive event formats, merging global music trends with regional cultural expectations. By 2020–2025, Oman Events had developed recurring flagship nights such as *88+ Wednesday Night*, *The Plug Friday*, and *Desert Bohemian Dip* at Crowne Plaza OCEC.

## Operations

Oman Events focuses on:

* **Nightlife programming** across Muscat's lounges

* **DJ rosters and local talent development**
* **Creative production**, including flyers, visuals, and digital media
* **Cultural alignment**, ensuring events meet Omani regulations and community expectations
